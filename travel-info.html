Getting to Albay

Air

There are short flights (roughly 75-90mins) from Manila to Legazpi in Albay, Naga City in Camarines Sur, Virac on Catanduanes and Masbate island. CALABARZON’s Quezon province is also a gateway to Bicol.

Land

If you prefer to travel by land, you can take a bus or drive to Albay. From Manila, it takes around 10 to 12 hours by bus. Several bus companies ply the Manila to Legazpi route, including: Cagsawa Travel and Tours, DLTBCo., Philtranco, Isarog Lines,Penafrancia Tours.

Within Albay

Once you arrive in Albay, you can easily travel around the province via tricycles, jeepneys, and buses. Tricycles are commonly available for short distances within towns and cities. Jeepneys and buses are the primary modes of transportation for traveling between towns and to other provinces in the Bicol Region.
